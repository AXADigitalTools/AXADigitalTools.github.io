Would you like to report this as a claim or are you just letting us know about the incident? i

If you think that the cost to repair your car will be less than your claim excess or you intend to claim directly from the person responsible for the damage then you do not need report this as a claim.

We will instruct our approved repairer to contact you and arrange for the damage to be assessed.
